# ByByte.DIY™

<img src="../img/logo.png" alt="ByByte.DIY logo" width="200">

### Open Robotics & STEM Education Ecosystem

ByByte.DIY™ is an open-source ecosystem for robotics, electronics, and STEM education.

We build:

* educational robotics platforms;
* open hardware;
* firmware and software tools;
* learning materials;
* and maker-friendly engineering projects.

Designed for:
students, educators, makers, robotics clubs, and STEM communities.

---

## Platforms

### ByByte Nano™

Compact beginner-friendly robotics platform.

### ByByte Mega™

Advanced modular platform for engineering and robotics projects.

### ByByte NanoBoy™

Portable experimentation and embedded learning platform.

---
